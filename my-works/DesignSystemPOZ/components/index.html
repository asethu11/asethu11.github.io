Colors

A four-step surface ladder that steps down into faint gray. Foreground in five shades for editorial hierarchy. One brand accent reserved for eight named surfaces. Status colors used for status, never as brand alternates.

Canvas + surface ladder

--canvas → --surface-4

Cards default to --surface-1. Featured cards lift one step to --surface-2. Deeper steps are for popover bg in dark mode and rare nested panels.
