Common questions

What clients ask first.

What if we don't know which engagement fits?

Start with a 30-minute call. We'll listen, ask three questions, and recommend the shape that fits the problem. There's no wrong starting point — we move between them.

Who actually does the work?

Senior practitioners, partner-led. No subcontracting, no offshore handoff. The pod that scopes the work ships the work.

How do the 92+ AI agents factor in?

They are the leverage. Reusable, domain-purpose agents do the heavy lifting so the human team focuses on the irreducible parts — strategy, judgment, design.

Do you sign NDAs and security agreements?

Yes. SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA-aligned. Mutual NDAs are standard before scoping calls.

What happens after the engagement ends?

You own everything — code, prompts, agents, system docs. We hand off cleanly, on the cadence you choose. Many clients roll on; many do not. Either is fine.

Can we hire your people directly?

No. The model is collective; the partnership is with Point One Zero. That's how we keep the lean structure that makes the work possible.
